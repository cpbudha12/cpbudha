Welcome to Lumbini

Lumbini is a sacred Buddhist pilgrimage located in the Rupandehi district of Nepal. It is the birthplace of the lord Buddha and is one of the most important historical sites for Buddhists around the world.

The site contains a number of temples, shrines, and monasteries, as well as the Mayadevi Temple, which is believed to be the exact spot where the Buddha was born. The site was designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1997.
